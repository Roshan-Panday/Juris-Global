Our Story
2009 — Founded
Adv. Vinay Kumar Dubey establishes Juris Global Legal Solutions in New Delhi, with a focus on property disputes and criminal defence in Delhi High Court.
2013 — Supreme Court Practice
First constitutional writ petition before the Supreme Court. The firm earns a reputation for complex multi-party property disputes.
2017 — NRI Division Launched
Dedicated NRI legal services division created to serve the Indian diaspora in the USA, UK, UAE, and Canada with property and family law matters.
2022 — Pan-India Expansion
Associates now appear before 50+ tribunals and High Courts across Delhi, Punjab & Haryana, Allahabad, Bombay, and Uttarakhand.
2025 — 2,800+ Cases Milestone
The firm crosses 2,800+ cases resolved with a 93% success ratio, now serving clients from several countries worldwide.
